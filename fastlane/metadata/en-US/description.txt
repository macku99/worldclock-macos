WorldClock is a beautiful macOS desktop widget that displays your local time alongside world clocks for any number of cities.

Key features:
- Stunning dark glassmorphism design that looks great on any wallpaper
- Local time displayed prominently with date and timezone info
- Unlimited world clocks with city name, current time, GMT offset, and hour difference
- Day-difference badges show "Tomorrow" or "Yesterday" when dates differ
- Supports half-hour and 15-minute timezone offsets
- Drag-to-reorder and manage your timezone list in the companion settings app
- Searchable timezone picker grouped by region
- Per-widget timezone customization — configure different clocks on each widget
- Extra large widget size for maximum visibility
- Automatic DST handling — times are always accurate
- All timezone data is on-device — no network connection required
